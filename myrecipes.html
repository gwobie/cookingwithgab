B

Broccoli Feta Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of chicken or vegetable broth or 4 cups of homemade broth
  • 2 Heads of broccoli (or one bag of frozen broccoli)
  • 3-4 Big garlic cloves
  • 2 sticks of celery (around 150 grams)
  • 125 grams of feta
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. On medium heat, add the oil and butter.
  2. Roughly chop up your onions, garlic and celery. Cook until the onions are translucent and smells fragrant. Add Brocoli, cook for a minute or 2.
  3. Cover the veggies in the broth, bring to a boil, then let simmer for 30 minutes, or until the broccoli is fully cooked.
  4. In the last 5 minutes, add your feta salt and pepper.
  5. Blend and enjoy!

R

Kalbi Ribs

Ingredients

  • 1 Rack of Ribs
  • ½ a Jar of Premade Kalbi sauce
  • 3 inch of Fresh Ginger
  • 4 or 5 cloves of Fresh Garlic
  • Green onions to your taste
  • 1 White Onion
  • 4 Celery Stalk
  • 2 Tablespoons of Soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon of Chili Garlic Paste

Steps

  1. Roughly cut your Green and White Onions, Celery, garlic and Ginger.
  2. Add your vegetables, Kalbi sauce, Soy Sauce, Garlic Chili paste to a large bag.
  3. Remove the membrane on your ribs and place ribs in the bag with the marinade.
  4. Let it marinade for a minimum of 4 hours or up to 24 hours.
  5. Preheat your oven to 250F.
  6. Place your ribs with the vegetables and sauce on a baking sheet lined with foil.
  7. Cook for 3 to 4 hours, and flip the ribs every 30 minutes.
  8. Broil the ribs for about 2 to 3 minutes uncovered.
  9. Enjoy!

S

Sourdough Loaf

  • Ingredients

    • 125grams starter
    • 325grams water
    • 500 grams of flour or bread flour
    • 10 grams of salt
    • Rice flour (optional, regular flour might burn while cooking the loaf, rice flour wont.)

      • Steps

    1. The next day mix the starter, water, salt, flour into a bowl until fully combined. Then let the dough rest for an hour.
    2. Do 4 sets of stretches and folds, 30 minutes apart.
    3. Shape your loaf on a floured surface (If you want to add any inclusions like cheese or garlic add them now). Let it rest for 15 minutes on the counter.
    4. Place into banneton. Cover it and place in fridge overnight or up to 3 days
    5. Preheat the oven to 450F.
    6. Score bread and place it on a parchment paper in your dutch oven.
    7. Cook for 40 minutes covered, then last 10-15 minutes uncovered or until golden brown.
    8. Let it cool for at least an hour or two before cutting.
    9. Enjoy!
